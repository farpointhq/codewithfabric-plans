Rollback-first design philosophy. Every stage ships with its rollback command tested on a preview before the forward deploy. We do not ship a stage whose rollback we haven't rehearsed. "Can we undo this in two minutes?" is the ship gate for stages 2 and 3 — if the answer is no, the plan is wrong and the stage must be re-scoped.
